Universal exchange token
You may ask: okay, this is pretty cool. However, wouldn't this result in rapid deflation of the universal exchange token since the only way it could possibly inflate is via the creation of a human being? To this, I say, yes, but that's the point. In a world of rapid deflation via innovation or efficiency, there is more competition to adapt that innovation or efficiency. The original inventor of the innovation or efficiency can still apply for a patent, which again, is controlled by the collective whole and signed by date and time. This encourages companies to pay increasingly large dividends to an increasingly large amount of investors decided in vote coins. If 100% of coins are placed in a "private" company that entirely provides food, you will get 100% food and 0% anything else. This encourages people to again, invest vote coins in ETFs that allow them to get the lifestyle they choose, and again, anyone can create an ETF, so people can plan vacations accordingly, for example. Real estate is solved too via rapid, rapid deflation. The universal exchange token can still be traded for smaller transactions, such as trading one token for a bottle of water. Again, the token can automatically change based on how much money, but the collective supply on the secure network would change based on the multiplier as well, preventing inflation as a whole. 
In a system of rapid, rapid deflation, no one goes hungry and no one leaves homeless. This is the modern combination of socialist and capitalist ideals. Each vote coin and universal exchange token is SUPPOSED to have much more purchasing power and much more value as a whole in the long run: that indicates real progress and real quality of life for the average person. There are no middlemen and no need for an independent calculator to measure how much money in supply must be created each year: it is entirely decided by how many humans there are (inflation) and the quality of life of each human (the purchasing power of the vote coin and the universal exchange token).
This goes into the entire idea of the collective whole (democracy) deciding meritocracy (capitalism). Workers do not seize the means of production: rather, the means of production are still delegated to the capitalist, just that they are heavily controlled by the collective whole, so the capitalist is incentivized to be a benevolent capitalist. This prevents monopolies because every single person has the potential to become a benevolent capitalist via their registering of a DPO. If every person can register a DPO, then it is still possible to sift through the chaos via implementing your own algorithm, such as top 500 companies in the US or top 100 companies registered in New York, etc. etc. Again, in a DPO offering, you invest vote coins, but they are not taken away from you and you cannot gain or lose any vote coins: every person invested in the ETF is literally equal if they invest a certain amount of coins.
